Windows Azure



Dronacharya College of Engineering, Gurgaon and MICROSOFT INNOVATION CENTRE jointly organized a WORKSHOP ON MICROSOFT AZURE in the college premises.Dronacharya being the Microsoft innovation centre helped to build and accelerate skills and provides support for building new ideas and organizes this seminar. The mega event which was spread over a span of 3 days from 18th-20th February, 2015 witnessed speakers from Microsoft along with participation from the innovative minds of Dronacharya. In the aforementioned workshop we learnt the following features about webpage and app development using azure and visual studio 2013.

Day 1:

The 1st day of the event was divided into 2 sessions: a seminar and lab session which was followed by expert MR. JAGDISH SASALA. The day began with a seminar on the subject “Developing websites and web pages on which data can be stored using SQL database and Microsoft's server”. This seminar provided attendees with all the tools you need to establish a vibrant, efficient and collaborative learning environment. By leveraging Folders to open channels of communication, files , Sites to share information with the community, and Forms to collect more, better and faster information which was followed by how to access and manage basic data in web pages using Microsoft Server . This was followed by a brief round of question answers. It was overall a very knowledgeable session and was very well received by the audience. On the whole Day 1 was a complete package for the students to familiarize them with what is MICROSOFT AZURE and how it will help to do web hosting ; what tools and technology can be used to bring their idea to real life.

Day 2:

The 2nd day of APP BUILDING WORKSHOP ON AZURE i.e. 19THFebruary , 2015’ saw active participation by students of all the branches of the college . The second day was focused on development of an app that can retrieve the data from the website or server.The basic idea behind the event was to bring out the innovation in the mind of the students and give them the opportunity to explore the real life applications of Engineering. The second day was completely a lab session; performing their ideas online using Azure. It was a fun developing such an app for ourselves.

Day 3:

The 3rd Day i.e. 20thFebruary , 2015 was the continuation of the second day. The third was on the target of developing two Apps : one for sending data and other one for retrieving data from the former and how it can be performed online using Microsoft server. At the end of the day there was a seminar on making website using visual studio2013, also website publishing using Filezilla software. It was followed up by a Question/ Answer round where MR. JAGDISH SASALA talked about how to start our own start up , use of BIZSPARK, and how Microsoft helps the upcoming Entrepreneurs and about sensors and Raspberry Pi and its future aspects.

A total of best 80 STUDENTS participated in the WORKSHOP. The 3 day workshop saw the active participation by students of all the branches and helped students to enhance their knowledge and think out of the box. The result of the 3 day workshop was that all the students have to work really hard to make their dreams come true & to achieve the desired results. Because “When a goal matters enough to a person, that person will find a way to accomplish what at first seemed impossible.”

Success isn't something that just happens - success is learned, success is practiced and then it is shared.
