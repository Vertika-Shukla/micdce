Microsoft Event 2014



Dronacharya College of Engineering, Gurgaon has organized a Microsoft Event on 13-Feburary -2014, in the Auditorium under the guidance and support of Prof Vishal Bharti, Head of Department- CSE & Dr. Jitender Kumar, Head of Department- IT.

The main objective of the Event was to help students in nurturing and harboring their Innovations, thus, implementing their ideas into products, giving information about initiative taken by Microsoft and how students can get benefits from this. This Session was organized by Microsoft IT Academy, under the support of Ms Sarita, Microsoft Coordinator from Department of CSE, Ms.Maneela Tuteja, MIC – Faculty Coordinator (IT) and student coordinators of CSE – Ms.Shilpa yadav (MSP), Mr.Jorawar Singh, Mr.Harsh Chawla, Mr.Bibhuti Bhushan, Mr.Deepak Chandel, Ms. Shivangi Shandilya & Mr.Akshit Chauhan.

“Aspire to Change the World” with these wordings of Satya Nadella (The CEO of Microsoft), Mr.Shivansh & Ms. Spardha, Student Coordinator – Department of CSE welcomed the guests and has delivered a presentation on Microsoft Session & its objectives. Dr. Jitender Kumar (HOD) IT, Head of Microsoft IT Academy also delivered a speech on Microsoft initiative taken by College. The details of eminent speakers in Event are as follows:

Mr. Mohit Chhabra, Presently working as software developer at Applied Information Sciences, Hyderabad .He is India's First Windows Azure Champion and Rockstar, Ambassador of Windows 7 & Mozilla, Imagine Cup Finalist, Winner of Yahoo Hack U and Microsoft Coding Competition at IIT Delhi and has trained more than 2000 students and professionals on Microsoft Technologies.

Mr. M.V Priyank Presently working as Digital Entrepreneur, Social Media Strategist, and Consultant (Advisor). He was the Part of MSP Social Media Team for Microsoft Imagine Cup Russia 2013, Microsoft Student Partner of NCR Delhi from 2010-2012, Regional Coordinator (Delhi Region) of SQLServerGeeks.com

This event highlighted the initiatives taken by the IT giant Microsoft such as “Project Jyoti” & “Project Shiksha” Windows U Crew, Microsoft Dream spark Yatra and the main part of this event was guiding the students in turning their dream into reality through app development, The Imagine Cup’14. Mr. M.V Priyank was one of the winners in Imagine Cup So he threw light on how to get the perfect balance between the logical implementation of the application and the Microsoft framework such as ASP.Net and Ms-Azure. Students came up with technical doubts and were clearly solved by the speakers. The Session was highly informative and a Motivation to the students who Dream Big!
