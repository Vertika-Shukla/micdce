Social Outreach



On 30th September 2014 , three students from Dronacharya College of Engineeirng of CSE-5th Semester namely Karan Bhalla , Deepak Chandel , Dhruv Pahuja went to Kulachi Hansraj Model School , Ashok Vihar ,New Delhi for Live streaming of ‘Microsoft Talent India’.

The main aim of conducting live streaming at school was to bring an exclusive opportunity to hear from a leader of the technology industry – Microsoft CEO, Satya Nadella. Education Minister Smriti Irani created awareness about ‘Women in IT’ and how women can lead in every field. Mr Satya Nadella discussed about the future of technology and its impact on students in India.

Event started with presentation of Bouqet to School Principal along with the staff members. Almost 30 girl students, Eight Teachers from school attended the event. MSA(Microsoft Student Associate) gave a small introduction of DCE,Gurgaon to the students of School. They also told about the MIC, Microsoft IT Academy, MOS & MTA certifications facility available at DCE, Gurgaon.

The Session had a great impact on school Students & CSE students also learned a lot.


School Visits

Ms. Megha Goel, MIC Coordinator, Dronacharya College of Engineering visited more 30 schools to spread awareness about various Microsoft Technologies, Initiatives and Awareness Programmes. She got an opportunity to meet the Principal, staff and other higher authorities and discussed the benefits. She also interacted with the students and spread words regarding various various Microsoft Certifications and programmes that can be done at their level.
