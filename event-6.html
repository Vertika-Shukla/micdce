Microsoft Student Partner (India) Summit 2014



“The only thing which we know about the Future is that it will be Different and the future belongs to those who prepare for it today “– Microsoft CEO,SatyaNadella at Microsoft Build Conference.These are the words not for motivation but to bring the future into reality and the future definitely belongs to Microsoft stating the fact that 2 in 7 people on the planet uses MS Office. More than 1.8billion people uses Windows OS every day, Skype users make an average of 50 billion minutes of calls per month,500 million active outlook.com users and the list of such facts is so long to understand that Microsoft is the future in software, electronics and hardware industry.

To teach this future into reality, Microsoft India organized Microsoft Student Partner (India) Summit 2014 for all Microsoft Student Associates (MSA) and Microsoft Student Partners (MSP) from 29 different states of India.The summit was held in the campus of Gyan Ganga Institute of Technology and Managementat City of Lakes – Bhopal from 08-10 November,2014 and comprised of knowledge and skill development sessions, discussions, workshops, and other learning and fun opportunities.

This year summit focused on following key themes:

1. Changing trends in the world related to services and products
2. Microsoft’s Big Data and Cloud services a boon to the world
3. The role that the MSAs and MSPs will together play for Microsoft
4. How Microsoft is Bringing future into reality

From 8th November 2014 to 10th November 2014 ,one of the faculty Ms.Sarita from CSE Department with six MSP namely Ayushi Mishra, Deepak Chandel, Dhruv Pahuja, Dipti Bhardwaj, Karan Bhalla and KunalAhujafrom Dronacharyacollege of Engineeirng, Gurgaon attended MSP Summit at Bhopal.

On 08- November 2014, Microsoft Summit started with a Lead Lab program and Youth Summit for the future technocrats who want to be entrepreneurs by Mr.SangeethVargese,Speaker-Gloabal Shapers Forum.There were some live demos in the session to learn entrepreneurial and management skills by heart. This session was followed by talks from Microsoft Executives and Industry Luminaries. The day ended with Cultural Night, where the Microsoft Country Head greeted all MSPs, VenkateshSarwasiddhi and MSP Summit Teaser was launched.

The Second day of Summit (November 09, 2014) commenced with the great energy and power packed participation in the Marathon by Adidas at 6.00 am. It was a 6km run for the boys and 4km run for the girls. Later, eligible MSPs registered for the FTE examsand gave the exams for internship, job opportunities. The day ended with DJ Night organized for all MSPs, and in the end of the day, all had a dinner.

On 10-November 2014,MSA & Faculty coordinator MsSarita had a lovely and useful interaction with Mr. Prabhjot Singh Bakshi and Mr. Aditya Mohan and discussed about various possibilities to our college as MIC.

Professionals from Microsoft India were praising Dronacharya College of Engineeirng, Gurgaon MIC (Microsoft Innovative Centre).Then, all MSPs had a Keynote address from the chief Guest, Joseph Landes, (General Manager- Developer Experience and Evangelism, Microsoft India). His remarkable persona and words enlightened each MSP present there to give the best of their capabilities and work for Microsoft.

The summit concluded by having Sessions on Windows Azure followed by MS Office 365.In the end there was a Session on Bing and Xbox Gaming.MSA from DCE, Gurgaon mingled and interacted with fellow MSPs and discussed on various Microsoft possibilities and opportunities.
