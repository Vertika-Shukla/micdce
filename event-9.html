MS-Excel Boot Camp



One Day Certification Boot Camp on Microsoft Excel on 23rd Jan 2015 was organized by Dronacharya College of Engineering for the First Year B.Tech students. Mr. Pankaj Prasad a trainer, guide and software associate from Cyber Learning, Gurgaon was the trainer for the workshop. He is a renowned Excel-2010 Champion for the year 2012.

The session was attended by 250 students of First Year. The Session was divided in two parts. In the first session Mr. Prasad introduced MOS Championship to the students. He started the training session by introducing MS-Excel 2010 to the students by creating simple Excel sheet and performing basic functions on it. Students took fruitful notes from the session. It was an interactive session, where in students ask lots of questions which were very deftly answered by the trainer.

Second session started with some advanced concepts of Excel-2010, where Mr. Prasad discussed about topics like hyperlink and formatting, removes personal information, conditional formatting, relative and absolute addressing, sorting and filtering, spark line. He also explained some mock questions which are helpful for the students for clearing the MOS test. In this session students were also made to perform task.
