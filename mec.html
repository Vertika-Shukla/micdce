Winning Referral Challenge



One of our MSP student Deepak Chandel got 3rd position all over India in MEC Competition by Microsoft Research India and is awarded with Windows Lumia 625 phone,eighteen thousand cheque,MEC Community Partner Plaque by Microsoft Research and appreciation letter. He attended the Microsoft TechVista 2015 on January 23,2015 at Bangalore.

As our student won MEC Challenge in driving Massively Empowered Classroom, a community initiative from Microsoft Research India. In recognition of our efforts and contribution, Dronacharya College of Engineering was as a “MEC Community Partner”.We had also received a plaque for the same.
