How technology has changed in the Last 20 years



On 17th October 2014, a Webinar for Female was organised in different labs of Dronacharya College of Engineering. The topic of Webinar was ‘How technology has changed in the last 20 years.

The webinar was hosted in different labs with girls’ students participating from CSE & IT branch. The webinar was conducted by three MSA’s from Dronacharya college of Engineering, namely, Karan Bhalla, Deepak Chandel and Dhruv Pahuja and 60 students attended the webinar.

Microsoft Women in Tech aims to empower you with the knowledge that will help explore the cool and fun aspects of Technology The main aim of the webinar series was to help bring some of the most exciting innovations in education led by Microsoft global experts to us. Microsoft EduCast brings together academic leaders, innovative institutions, and pioneering educators in a monthly series for educators.

Main topic of Discussion was “how communications have changed in the past 20 years”. Communications has changed in three major ways: speed, modality, and location. We expect and need communication to be instant these days and want several different modalities (voice, video, e-mail, IM) to get a hold of someone. In addition, physical location has become irrelevant due to the Internet.

The faculty of CSE department proved to be a great support for conducting the session. All the female staff of CSE Department attended the webinar in a separate lab with full enthusiasm to lend their helping hands for women security and women empowerment.
